Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Get Started" and follow the registration process. You'll need to verify your email with an OTP code.

Is my medical data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and follow HIPAA guidelines to protect your health information.

Can I share my records with doctors?

Absolutely! You have full control over who can access your medical records.

How do I book an appointment?

Log in to your account, search for doctors, and book an appointment directly through the platform.
