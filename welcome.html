About MedVault

MedVault is a revolutionary Personal Electronic Health Record (EHR) System designed to empower patients with complete control over their medical data. Founded with a mission to make healthcare more accessible and transparent, we're bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers.

Our platform enables secure storage, easy sharing, and efficient management of medical records. With features like appointment booking, health reminders, and real-time notifications, we're committed to improving your healthcare experience.
